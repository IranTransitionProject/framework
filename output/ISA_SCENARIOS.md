### Observation 002: Iran's Structural Advantages Are Real (v1.0)
* **Diagnosis:** In every negative comparison case (Iraq, Libya, Syria, Afghanistan), at least two of the following were absent: strong national identity, educated population, institutional bureaucratic depth, energy resource base.
